About Us

St lawrence University(SLAU) is
the private universty located in Mengo Rubaga Division, in kampala,
the capital city of uganda. the university was found in 2006.
and was launched by H.E Yoweri Kaguta museveni,
the president of the Republic of Uganda on 14th october 2006
under the proprietorship of the late professor Lawrence Mukiibi.
the Universty took in the first class of students in september 2007.
